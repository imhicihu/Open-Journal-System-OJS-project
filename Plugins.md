* Browse to the `Settings` > `Websites` > `Plugins` > `Plugin Gallery` page in your Open Journal Systems website setup.
* Enable this plugins:
     * iThenticate
     * QuickSubmit
     * [Allowed Uploads](https://github.com/ajnyga/allowedUploads)
     * Custom Header
     * [backup](https://github.com/asmecher/backup)
	 * Google Analytics
	 * DOI
